What to Expect

We are a loving community of fellow believers. We pray for eachother, we worship together, we seek knowledge, and we seek to live out the mission of Jesus.

About Us

We are a new church in the Bellevue/West Nashville area that exists to bring Glory to God and to make Him known. We would love to meet you, encourage you, and support you through your walk with Christ!

Get Involved

We meet every Wednesday for Life Groups at the Hampton Inn Bellevue conference room. We gather for supper, Bible study, and fellowship. Cost for the meal is $5 for adults; kids eat free. All are welcome!

What We Believe

GOD the FATHER

We believe that there is one God who exists in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

GOD the SON (Jesus)

We believe that Jesus is the Son of God, was born of a virgin, was fully God and fully human. He lived a sinless life, died in our place as a sacrifice for the sins of humanity, was buried, arose bodily from the grave, and ascended into Heaven.

GOD the HOLY SPIRIT

We believe that God the Holy Spirit is active in convincing unbelievers of their need for salvation. He is the Comforter and Guide Who lives in every believer.

THE BIBLE

We believe that the Bible is the Word of God without any error, the sole authority for life and faith.

HUMANITY

We believe that humanity is a special creation of God, made in His image. Through the sin of the first man, Adam, humanity fell so that all people are sinners and need salvation.

SALVATION

We believe that salvation is a gift received through repentance toward God and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.

THE CHURCH

We believe that the church is a local body of baptized believers with the Lord Jesus Christ as the Head.

PRAYER

We believe that each believer has direct access to God through the Lord Jesus Christ.

New Day Church is a cooperating Southern Baptist Church that affirms the Baptist Faith and Message 2000.
