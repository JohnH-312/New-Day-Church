Our Ministries

  • Small Group

    While we have moved locations for Sunday Worship, we are still meeting every Wednesday for Small Group at the River Plantation Section IV Clubhouse. We gather for supper, Bible study, and fellowship. Cost for the meal is $5 for adults; kids eat free. All are welcome!

  • New Day Kids

    Kids are welcome to join our service, but we also offer a place for children to play and learn the Word of God from one of our Family Volunteers.

  • Young Adult

    Our Young Adults range from college age to 30s, and love to have fun! We have done book studies, game nights, and nights out to bowl or other events! Check out what our next event will be!

  • Community Events

    New Day is thrilled to host events for the Bellevue Community throughout the year. Our main event in the spring is an Easter Egg Hunt held at Red Caboose/Bellevue Park the Saturday before Easter. Our fall event is a Fall Festival, also held at Red Caboose Park. Stay tuned to our page and social media for updates and news on those events and more!
